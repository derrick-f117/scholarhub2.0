How it works

1. Signing up

This a very simple process that'll take you five minutes at most. All you have to do is create an account, enter a password, and you're all done! See? Simple!

2. Getting started

Here on scholarHuB, we have a wide selection of examinations, short tests, notes, and virtual laboratories you can chose from. All of these will help you keep occupied on more constructive activities over the holidays.

All your progress will be saved on our servers and displayed to you through our comprehensive student dashboard. All you have to do is pick something to do and you're all set!

4. Dashboard

Our student dashboard helps you keep track of your performance on a daily basis.

a. Your name

This displays the user name you enterd during the sign-in process

b. Your score

This displays your current score, the score is based on the number of successfully completed activities.

c. Virtual labs completed

This displays the number of virtual laboratories you have successfully completed.

d. In progress

This displays your ongoing activities and the progress you have made in each.

Titles

Each and every month, we declare our top student of the month. This is the scholarHuB student who has managed to accumulate the highest number of points in each month.
